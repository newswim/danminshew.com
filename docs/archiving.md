# Digital footprint archival (adjacent track)

Goal: preserve the records and audio of Dan's musical and online history before platforms lose them — then surface the best of it on /music.

## ⚠️ Urgency: MySpace

MySpace **already lost roughly 12 years of user-uploaded music (2003–2015, ~50M songs) in a 2019 server migration**. Anything still playable there survived by luck and should be treated as at-risk. If a band page still streams recordings, capture it **now**, not during some future archiving sprint.

Research (2026-07-18) found both Fight Pretty MySpace pages **live with full track metadata**, but audio did not stream in anonymous testing — the player queues tracks yet never fetches an audio source. Either the streams are gone (2019-loss casualty) or anonymous playback is broken. **Next cheap experiment: retest playback while logged in to MySpace.** If any stream resolves, capture same-day (yt-dlp or browser network capture), plus full-page snapshots for artwork/blurbs/comments.

## Inventory (from research; Dan to correct/extend)

### Fight Pretty — chaotic hardcore, Richwood TX, ~2005–2009

- Discogs: https://www.discogs.com/artist/12851345-Dan-Minshew — releases "Raised By Anger" (2005), "Rewiring The Human Body" (2007, Dwell Records)
- **MySpace #1: https://myspace.com/fightpretty** — 9 tracks spanning releases *not on Discogs*: Nesting Demo ("48th and airport"), Smoke Filled Womb (5 tracks), Ghost Town Electric split ("Nightwalkers"), Androphobia 7" — the most at-risk, least-documented material
- **MySpace #2: https://myspace.com/fight.pretty** — full "Rewiring the Human Body" (10 tracks, 2007 + 2013 re-upload)
- Press surviving: Lambgoat review, rockfreaks.net review, AllMusic entry, darklyrics lyric archive
- April 2009 free-download release of the album — original link almost certainly dead
- PureVolume: dead platform, no Wayback capture found under /fightpretty — did the band use another handle?

### Dear Human — Denton TX instrumental/math rock, ~2008–2010

- Bandcamp (stable, CC BY-NC, downloadable): https://dearhuman.bandcamp.com/ — s/t album, 2010-03-26, **Gutterth Records** (GR021). Dan on guitar (L), with Casey Nalley, Michael Scroggins, John Gillespie; mixed with Michael Briggs.
- **MySpace: https://myspace.com/dearhuman — 4 tracks exist nowhere else**: "waiting - EARLY DEMO" (2009), "kareem octopus - DEMO" (2009), "nomadic music box - DEMO" (2009), and "Nomadic (Live from RGRS)" (2008, from MySpace-only release "Andrew's iPhone"). Same playback caveat. Fresh Wayback snapshots submitted 2026-07-18.
- Spotify artist page confirmed; Violitionist Session recorded March 2010 (by Michael Briggs) — ask Violitionist/Briggs about session masters.
- **dearhuman.org is lost** — lapsed, parked for sale on GoDaddy. Historical content is Wayback-only.
- Check Gutterth compilation albums (gutterth.bandcamp.com) for Dear Human appearances.
